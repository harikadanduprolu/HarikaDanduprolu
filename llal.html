Dear Candidates,

We are excited to offer you the opportunity to showcase your event management skills through an Event Planning Task. This task will help us assess your creativity, planning, and organizational abilities.

Event Concept:

Choose an event (e.g. hackathon, workshop, conference) and define its purpose, target audience, and expected outcomes. The event should be relevant to our college.

Event Plan:

  • Theme & Agenda: Develop a theme and a detailed agenda, including potential speakers or activities.
  • Budget: Plan a budget with a limit of Rs. 10,000, covering essential costs like tools, fees, travel costs, food for guests, etc.
  • Logistics: Identify the necessary tools for smooth event execution.
  • Marketing Strategy: Outline how you will promote the event and attract attendees.
  • Team Roles: Define roles for a hypothetical team (e.g. event coordination, tech support).
  • Risk Management: Provide a brief plan for handling common risks.

Submission Guidelines:

Submit your event plan as a document or PowerPoint presentation, covering all aspects from concept to execution.

Presentation and Interview:

If selected for the next round, you will present your event plan to a panel and participate in a short interview to discuss your ideas and strategies in more detail.

Deadline: 25th September

Please note that your event plan should be specific to VNRVJIET.

We look forward to seeing your event ideas! Good luck!
